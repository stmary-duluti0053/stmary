Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Must have completed primary education.
  • Must meet the age requirements for the grade level.
  • Must pass the entrance examination (if applicable).
  • Other eligibility criteria as specified by the school’s policies.

Application Process

The application process is simple and can be done online through our website or in person at the school’s administrative office. Follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Fill out the online application form.
  • Step 2: Submit necessary documents (birth certificate, school reports, etc.).
  • Step 3: Schedule and attend an interview (if applicable).
  • Step 4: Await the admission decision.

Please provide the following information during the application process:

  • Full name (as per the birth certificate)
  • Date of birth
  • Address and contact details
  • Previous school details (if applicable)
