Welcome to the Admission Application Portal

We are delighted that you are considering St. Mary's Duluti for your educational journey. Our institution is committed to nurturing talent, fostering excellence, and preparing students for a bright future.

St. Mary’s Duluti is pleased to announce that applications are now open for the 2025 academic year for all levels — from Form One to Form Six. We warmly invite students and parents to apply and become part of a school where excellence, discipline, and personal growth are at the heart of everything we do.

How to Apply:

  1. Select the class level you wish to apply for (e.g., Form One, Form Five).
  2. A message will appear prompting you to make a payment for the application form.
  3. Complete the payment using your mobile number.
  4. You will receive a verification code via SMS after the payment.
  5. Enter the verification code in the space provided.
  6. Fill in your personal and academic information accurately to complete the application.
